Reproduction

GRPO's ranking, reproduced

A minimal, CPU-only study that recovers the qualitative ranking from Figure 5 of DeepSeekMath (Shao et al., 2024): RFT / Online RFT / GRPO+OS at 0.18 / 0.95 / 0.99 on a synthetic verifiable-reward task (mean of 3 seeds), in under a minute. RFT, Online RFT, and GRPO+OS share one training loop.
